The Perfect Buttercream frosting recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of butter, room tempature.
  • 7 cups of powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 – 1/3 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  1. In the bowl of your stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, beat the butter on medium speed until smooth. Turn the speed to low and slowly add the powdered sugar until it’s mixed in.
  2. Slowly stream in 1/4 cup of the heavy cream and vanilla. Turn the mixer up to medium speed and mix for 1 minute until creamy. Add additional heavy cream, if a creamier frosting is desired.
